1. What is Health and Physical Education?

Health and Physical Education is a field of study that focuses on the development of physical fitness, mental well-being, and overall health. It aims to educate individuals on the importance of exercise, healthy eating, hygiene, and mental health. The subject promotes physical activities such as sports, yoga, and fitness exercises to improve endurance, strength, flexibility, and coordination.

Key benefits of Health and Physical Education include:

  • Enhances physical fitness and overall health.
  • Develops teamwork and leadership skills.
  • Promotes mental well-being and stress management.
  • Encourages lifelong healthy habits.
  • Improves concentration and academic performance.
