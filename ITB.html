Characteristics of Business

Business involves the production, distribution, and exchange of goods and services to meet human needs. Its key characteristics include:

  • Economic Activity
  • Profit Motive
  • Risk and Uncertainty
  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Dynamic Environment

Importance of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

CSR refers to businesses contributing to social and environmental well-being. Importance includes:

  • Improves Brand Image
  • Enhances Customer Loyalty
  • Boosts Employee Morale
  • Ensures Sustainable Development
Need for Business Environment

The business environment includes all external factors that impact business operations. Key reasons why it is important:

  • Helps in Strategic Planning
  • Identifies Market Trends
  • Encourages Adaptability
  • Enhances Competitive Advantage
Types of Entrepreneurs

Entrepreneurs can be classified based on various factors such as innovation, risk-taking, and business scale. Common types include:

  • Innovative Entrepreneurs
  • Imitative Entrepreneurs
  • Social Entrepreneurs
  • Technopreneurs
  • Scalable Startup Entrepreneurs
Liberalization, Privatization, and Globalization (LPG)

LPG reforms opened up economies worldwide, driving trade and investments.

  • Liberalization: Removing trade barriers, reducing government control.
  • Privatization: Transfer of government-owned businesses to private hands.
  • Globalization: Integration of economies through trade, technology, and culture.
Merger, Takeover, and Amalgamation Strategies

Businesses use these strategies to grow and remain competitive.

  • Merger: Two companies join to form one entity.
  • Takeover: One company acquires another.
  • Amalgamation: Combination of multiple companies to form a new entity.
